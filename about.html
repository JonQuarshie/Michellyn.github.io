About Us

Transforming lives through the saving knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ.
FOUNDER

And you shall love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind and with all your strength. Mark 12:30

Lady Evangelist Michaelina Anaman has a great passion for soul winning. She started singing in Church and School at age 5. Over the years, she kept singing and developing her God-given gifts. Her evangelism ministry began following a message preached by her Pastor on, “What shall I render to the Lord for all His benefits?” She felt she was being a selfish Christian, so she took it upon herself to preach on the bus on her way to work. On the 10th August, 2015, she did her first bus evangelism and 4 souls were won to the Glory of God. The Lord kept building her up and by God’s grace, in February 2016 she started this Ministry full-time.

Evangelist Michaelina Anaman holds a Second-Class Upper Division (HND) from Accra Technical University as a Building Technologist. and has worked with NGO's and regious institutions in Ghana.
