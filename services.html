Our Programmes and Activities

Over the years the Ministry has reached out to many people of diverse backgrounds. Our programmes include
  • Street evangelism
  • Prison Visitation and Donation
  • Jesus Touch Crusade
  • Psychiatric Evangelism

Street evangelism

In our believe of preaching and spreading the Gospel of Hope in Jesus Christ to people of all walks of life, we present the message of the cross into every city and town. This we do through bus preaching, dawn broadcasts, invading the streets and marketplaces to preach and share Bible tracts, bumper stickers, going from shop to shop to spread the word of God,

engaging in one-on-one evangelism to win souls for God. This was our maiden programme which started in 2015 with bus evangelism and dawn broadcasts. It has since been extended to the streets.

Prison Visitation and Donation

As the Bible says in Matthew 25: 36b; “when I was in prison, you visited me”, we extended our outreach to the Prisons to send God’s love and word to the inmates in the various prisons and to give them hope.

We have Service with them and make donations. We have so far visited the Nsawam and Tarkwa Prisons. Our first visitation was in April 2018.

Jesus Touch Crusade

In Mathew 28:19 the Bibles says, “Therefore go and make disciples of all nations..”. This programme is organised to reach out to people in the communities with the main aim of bringing salvation,

healing and deliverance to God’s people more especially in the rural areas, and then extended to the urban and even the diaspora. It is mostly a 2-night programme. On 24th and 25th of May, 2018, we had our first Jesus Touch Crusade.

Psychiatric Evangelism

It has been our firm believe through the inspiration and leading of the Holy Spirit to share the love, hope and healing power of the Lord Jesus Christ to our brethren in the Psychiatric hospitals.

We do this through visitations and spending quality time of worship with our brothers and sisters in the various psychiatric hospitals, praying with them and also making donations to them. We have received testimonies of massive discharges and healing in the Institutions after our visits. We organised our first Psychiatric Evangelism on 16th July, 2016.
