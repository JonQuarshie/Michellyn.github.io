About Michellyn-ministries

Founded in february 2016 by worship minstrel and lady evangelist Michaelina Anaman. We are dedicated to evangelizing to people of diverse backgrounds, teaching about the love of God with nuturing encouragement in preparation for the second coming.

we driven by the vision to- provide hope to the hopeless by evangelizing to all mankind, To teach the love of Jesus Christ to all mankind and prepare them for His Second coming. And to provide financial and technical support to churches in the rural areas of Ghana to complete their church buildings.
